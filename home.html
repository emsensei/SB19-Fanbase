SB19 PAGTATAG!

Out now on all streaming platforms!

SB19 is going to have a world tour for their upcoming EP (extended play) "PAGTATAG!" The P-pop group made the announcement on Twitter, Sunday, sharing the schedules and locations for the shows. SB19 will kick off the tour in Manila on June 24-25, and then they will head to North America in July and August. They will stop in various cities in the United States and Canada, among them Chicago, Dallas, San Francisco, Los Angeles, New York, Washington DC, Toronto, Winnipeg, Vancouver, and Edmonton.
