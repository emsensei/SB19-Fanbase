What is SB19?

SB19 is a five-member boy group under 1Z Entertainment. The group debuted on October 26, 2018 with their first debut digital single, "Tilaluha". Their name stands for 'Sound Break 19'.

“SB” in SB19 came from the initials of ShowBT because the group is the first Pinoy idol group in their company, while “19” is derived from the Philippines’ (+63) and South Korea’s (+82) country codes. When each number sums up (6+3+8+2), they get 19. This signifies the collaboration between South Korea and Philippines.

SB19 eventually came up with a new meaning which is "Sound Break" because the main goal of the group is to break into the music scene with their "New" and "Fresh" sound

C A R E E R

2018: Pre-debut, Official debut and first fanmeeting event

In May 2018, ShowBT Philippines announced that they will have their first Filipino idol group to debut in 2018. On May 16, 2018 the members of the group were revealed through the Official SNS accounts of the group. The group consists of Sejun (now known as Pablo),Josh,Stell,Ken, and Justin. On June 15, 2018, the group’s Official Twitter and Instagram accounts were opened.

The group announced that they will officially make their debut in the month of October. On October 18, 2018, the group announced their upcoming debut single and its release date, the single titled “Tilaluha” will be released on October 26, 2018.On October 26, 2018, the group officially made their debut with their first debut single “Tilaluha”. On November 27, 2018, the group announced that they are holding their first fanmeeting and mini concert on December 19, 2018.


2019: "Go Up", rising popularity, Fandom name and "Alab (Burning)"

SOn July 10, 2018, the group announced that they would be making their first comeback single, the name of their comeback single was titled Go Up. The single is set to release in July 19, 2019.

On September 2, 2018, a Twitter user uploaded the group’s Go Up Dance practice video, his uploaded video went viral. The group gained public attention after a year and more than that they were unrecognized by the people.

On November 3, 2019, the group announced on their Facebook live their official fandom name and it was A’TIN. The group's fandom name is derived from the Filipino word "Atin", pronounced as "ey-teen" which means "ours".

On December 25, Sony Music Philippines announced that SB19 had signed a recording contract with them, and on the same day, the group released their third single, "Alab (Burning)", under the new record label.

2020: Get In The Zone

On July 31, 2020, the group released their first full-length album Get In The Zone with “Hanggang Sa Huli” serving as the album’s title track.

The track Love Goes is an unrelease song of the group, the song was also first performed on Aja-Aja Tayo Season 2 in 2019,and the track

"Wag Mong Ikunot Ang Iyong Noo" was only released on the group's SNS accounts on December 5, 2019.


2021: "What?", "Mapa", "Mapa (Band Version)", Pagsibol, Love at First Stream OST, Ken (FELIP) Solo Debut, and "Ligaya"

On March 9, 2021, the group released their 4th single "What?" as the first pre-release single on their upcoming first EP.[21]

On May 16, 2021, the group released the second pre-release single "Mapa" for the group’s upcoming first EP.A Band Version was released on June 27, 2021 with Ben&Ben

On July 22, 2021, the group officially released their first EP Pagsibol.

As part of the celebration of their 3rd anniversary, SB19 released a 6-episode series featuring the group members each, released on WeTV and YouTube.They also held the SB19 2021: Our Zone Third Anniversary Concert on December 18 and 19 streamed live online and later released on WeTV.

On September 4, 2020, the group released their first promotional single for Dunkin ’Donuts titled, "The One.

On September 10, 2021, a teaser photo was posted on SB19's social media accounts announcing that Ken will be making his debut as a soloist under 'FELIP' with his single 'Palayo' on September 18, 2021

On December 17, 2021, the group released an OST single “No Stoppping You” for the 2021 film “Love at First Stream”.

On December 18, 2021, the group released their first special christmas single, “Ligaya”.
